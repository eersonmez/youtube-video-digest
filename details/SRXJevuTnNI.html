What This Video Is About

This video details how The Private Equity Group (TPG) has integrated OpenAI's ChatGPT into its investment research processes to achieve significant operational efficiencies and cost reductions. By being early adopters of AI, TPG leverages direct access to OpenAI licenses distributed through the ORC. The core theme is demonstrating a practical application of generative AI in high-stakes financial analysis, specifically within market studies and due diligence workflows.

The adoption of ChatGPT has allowed TPG to accelerate its understanding of various industries and investment spaces. Beyond general information gathering, the firm has moved into more specialized applications by developing customized skills and GPT models trained on their proprietary internal data. This internal development is key to rapidly analyzing complex financial metrics like unit economics and streamlining the review of extensive data rooms.
