Bain Capital is leveraging ChatGPT to drive automation and efficiency within its portfolio companies, specifically Zealus.

OpenAI · 2026-07-08T22:51:23+00:00 · How Bain Capital Is Scaling AI with ChatGPT

What This Video Is About

This video discusses how Bain Capital is leveraging enterprise-level adoption of ChatGPT across its entire workforce to drive significant automation and efficiency gains within its portfolio companies. The central focus is on Zealus, one of Bain Capital's major investments, which operates as a large clearing house managing payment claims processing between payers and providers.

Zealus handles an immense volume of financial transactions, processing hundreds of billions of dollars in annual claims. Historically, this process has been highly manual and labor-intensive, leading to various issues such as kickbacks. The integration of AI is fundamentally changing this operational landscape by automating these complex processes. Bain Capital believes this technological shift not only promises substantial cost reductions for Zealus but also signals a broader positive impact on the entire healthcare industry.

Detailed Walkthrough

The discussion begins with the scale of adoption, noting that having 2,700 employees who all possess an enterprise ChatGPT license has acted as a "giant force multiplier" for the business [0:00]. The speaker then introduces Zealus, describing it as one of Bain Capital's largest portfolio companies in which they have been invested since 2019 [0:09].

Zealus functions at the intersection of payment claims processing. It serves as a very large clearing house, situated between payers on one side and providers on the other to facilitate claim processing [0:17]. The sheer scale of this operation is massive, with the business processing hundreds of billions of dollars in claims annually, involving hundreds of millions of individual claims [0:26]. This high volume inherently creates potential issues, such as kickbacks.

The core application of AI here is to automate these processes, moving away from what was previously a "highly manual, highly labor intensive way" [0:35]. The expected outcomes are twofold: achieving cost takeout for Zealus itself and realizing cost takeout benefits across the entire healthcare industry [0:44]. The speaker concludes with strong confidence in the readiness of both the underlying AI models and the enterprises to embrace this technological shift, expressing excitement about their partnership with OpenAI [0:52].
