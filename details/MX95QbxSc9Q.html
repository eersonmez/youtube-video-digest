What This Video Is About

This video discusses the transformative potential of AI agents for large enterprises, as presented by Barak Kaufman from Wonderful. The central theme is that organizations should focus their efforts on organizational transformation using AI rather than attempting to build the underlying AI infrastructure themselves. Wonderful positions itself as an enterprise applied AI partner that provides a platform designed specifically to help companies build, manage, and optimize these sophisticated agents.

The discussion moves beyond simple task automation; it addresses a fundamental shift in how enterprises should operate moving forward. The current phase of AI development allows for much more complex applications—specifically, longer-horizon agentic tasks involving multiple interacting agents and various modalities. This represents a strategic pivot toward rethinking core business processes to optimize overall productivity for the future.
