Anthropic presents Claude Fable 5 as a highly capable model with stricter cyber and biology safeguards.

Anthropic · 2026-06-09T17:00:05+00:00 · Introducing Claude Fable 5

What This Video Is About

This video introduces Claude Fable 5, which Anthropic presents as its most capable model released to the public date. Fable 5 is described as a Mythos-class system that has been engineered with enhanced safeguards to make it suitable for general use. The announcement addresses the limitations of previous high-capability models, specifically mentioning "Claude Mythos preview," which was withheld from broad release because testing revealed its ability to discover thousands of cybersecurity vulnerabilities—a dual-use risk capable of both defensive and offensive exploitation.

The core purpose of Fable 5 is to provide users with access to a highly powerful AI while mitigating significant risks associated with advanced capabilities in sensitive domains like cyber security or biology. Anthropic positions Fable 5 as an answer that allows for high capability while maintaining safety, enabling it to tackle complex, long-horizon tasks across various professional fields without the inherent dangers of its predecessor.

Detailed Walkthrough

The presentation begins by announcing Claude Fable 5 as the most capable model released publicly [0:02]. A key feature of Fable 5's safeguards is its automatic review process for requests involving high-risk areas such as cybersecurity or biology [0:53-1:00]. When a request falls into these categories, the system intentionally redirects it to Opus 4.8 [1:00-1:02]. This mechanism allows users to leverage the capabilities of a powerful model like Fable without exposing themselves to the inherent cyber and biology risks associated with such advanced models [1:05-1:11]. Anthropic notes that these safeguards are currently broad but will continue to be refined to better permit safe requests [1:13-1:17].

Fable 5 is designed for ambitious, complex work. It possesses the ability to maintain focus on a problem for an extended duration compared to prior models and operates with high autonomy, capable of functioning for days without human intervention [1:20-1:28]. Its utility extends beyond coding; it can manage projects in finance, research, economics, law, and other complicated tasks that previously required constant supervision [1:30-1:36]. The concluding thought is to direct users toward applying Fable 5 to significant problems worth solving [1:39-1:48].
