What This Video Is About

This video outlines a program designed to assist veterans in navigating career transitions by leveraging practical Artificial Intelligence (AI) skills taught through the OpenAI Academy in collaboration with Veterans Forge. The central premise is that AI acts as a significant equalizer, providing individuals with opportunities regardless of their prior background or formal education.

The core objective for participants is to construct an "intelligence file." This file is intended to be comprehensive enough to apply to virtually any job, allowing the veteran to gain a deep understanding of their own professional ethos and capabilities. The session emphasizes that attendees will depart not just with knowledge, but with tangible, usable skills relevant to modern career paths, whether those goals involve starting a business or reaching executive levels.
