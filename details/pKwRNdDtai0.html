ChatGPT plugins connect apps and role-specific skills to support workplace workflows.

OpenAI · 2026-07-21T18:00:39+00:00 · Plugins in ChatGPT

What This Video Is About

The video presents ChatGPT Plugins as a way to connect ChatGPT with workplace applications such as Gmail, Slack, Google Drive, email, documents, calendars, Canva, and other tools. The stated purpose is to help individuals and teams get more work done by bringing relevant information and task execution into a ChatGPT conversation.

A plugin does more than link an application: it can also include “skills,” described as playbooks that guide ChatGPT through particular types of work. Examples include structuring a report, preparing for a meeting, or following a repeatable workflow. The video frames this as a way to use familiar applications while interacting through a conversational interface.

Two demonstrations show the pattern in practice. Canva is used to create an event flyer from a natural-language request, while a sales plugin gathers information from Gmail, Google Drive, and Slack to prepare for a customer review. In both cases, ChatGPT helps gather or create material, and the result can be reviewed or passed back into an existing workplace tool.
