Machiavelli emphasizes that true freedom is not merely the absence of a single executioner who can summarily kill an individual on the street. Instead, liberty exists within a system that requires due process, including trials and public examination before any action is taken against a citizen. The Florentines deeply valued this tradition of liberty, symbolized by their banner 'libertas,' which was associated with the Senate, composed of the top one percent of the elite. This sense of freedom was so significant to them that even the most benevolent tyrant would not be able to erase it entirely from their cultural understanding.

Metabolic Warning Sign Hiding in a Gout test (Uric Acid)

The video discusses how uric acid, often only associated with gout, is actually one of the earliest indicators that a person's metabolism is deteriorating, potentially years before blood sugar abnormalities appear. While standard lab ranges (below 6.8 or 7 mg/dL) are for gout, optimal levels for cardiometabolic health and insulin resistance should be below 5 mg/dL, with women possibly needing levels under 4 mg/dL. High uric acid is primarily driven by fructose and added sugars in modern diets, not just red meat. Catching this early is crucial because metabolic issues are easier to reverse before permanent damage occurs from conditions like high blood pressure or heart disease.

  • Uric acid can predict diabetes and insulin resistance years in advance.
  • Optimal uric acid for metabolic health is below 5 mg/dL, not just the gout threshold.
  • Fructose and added sugars are major drivers of elevated uric acid.
  • Early intervention with high uric acid improves long-term health span.
  • Metabolic damage can occur before A1C levels show issues.
