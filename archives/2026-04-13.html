This discussion with Dr. Natalie Crawford emphasizes proactive steps women can take to enhance their reproductive and hormonal health. Key recommendations include getting an AMH test to understand egg reserve, recognizing chronic inflammation or insulin resistance as potential warning signs, and prioritizing cycle tracking to listen to hormonal cues. The conversation highlights that lifestyle factors significantly impact fertility; for instance, avoiding NSAIDs around ovulation is advised as they can prevent follicle rupture.
