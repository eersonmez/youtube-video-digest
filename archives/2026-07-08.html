Why Some Zigbee Devices Work So Much Better

This video explains that the poor performance or limited usability of some Zigbee devices within Home Assistant is rarely an issue with the protocol itself. Instead, it stems from a lack of robust ecosystem support across three layers: the manufacturer, the community, and the software maintainers. The speaker contrasts Tuya devices, which often feel like gambling due to inconsistent functionality, with Sonoff devices that benefit from official custom quirks provided by the vendor. These official quirks significantly enhance device capabilities beyond basic pairing, transforming partially useful hardware into genuinely valuable components for complex automations. Viewers are advised to evaluate ecosystem support rather than just price or specs when purchasing Zigbee gear.
